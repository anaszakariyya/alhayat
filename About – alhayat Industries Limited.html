About us

Al-hayat industries limited in manufacturing of Polyethylene extrusion, cutting and printing in Nigeria with manufacturing facilities based in sharada phase 3.

Al-hayat universal industry limited  located in kano State, Nigeria. 

Al-hayat is an environmentally friendly company who provides extruded blown film for a variety of applications. The company was founded in 2002 by our Managing Director, Alh. Zakariyya Abubakar The company’s commitment, hard work and ambition have made it grow manifold and today and we successfully service 5000 customers annually. Emphasis is placed, above all, on customer satisfaction through delivery of high-quality products.

Client Comes First

Quality Products

Al-hayat products always meet customer expectation hence our customers total satisfaction and long-term loyalty. We do this through usage of world-class machinery, quality raw materials, meticulous quality control and a team of highly technical skilled staff.
