About us

Al-hayat industry limited in manufacturing of Polyproduct, cutting and printing in Nigeria with manufacturing facilities based in sharada phase 3.

 

Al-hayat is an environmentally friendly company which provides a variety of applications. The company was founded in 2008 by our Managing Director, Alh. Zakariyya Abubakar The company’s commitment, hard work and ambition have made it grow and today we successfully service 5000 customers annually. Emphasis is placed, above all, on customer satisfaction through delivery of high-quality products.

Client Comes First

In our business we pride ourselves on customers satisfaction, we always put customers requirements first.

Reliable Processes

Our process is consistently compliant with our quality management system, the trusted industrial standard.

Quality Products

Al-hayat products always meet customer expectation hence our customers total satisfaction and long-term loyalty. We do this through usage of world-class machinery, quality raw materials, meticulous quality control and a team of highly technical skilled staff. 

Commitment to delivery

We believe that commitment to delivery is of pivotal importance as it boosts the client’s confidence in us.
